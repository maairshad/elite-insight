Elite Insight - Batch O/L 1994, A/L 1997

Inception of Organization

Help of almighty Allah, Elite insight organization was officially unveiled on Wednesday 18-03-2018. Formed a concept in the mind and hands together of OL & AL batches, years 94 / 97 respectively. It was initiated by students who studied together during the same period from several schools such as As-siraj Mahavidyalaya, Al-Fayesha Maha Vidyalaya and Muslim Central College in Akkaraipattu Region, Sri Lanka.
